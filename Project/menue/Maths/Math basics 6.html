Rectangular coordinate system

Definition
  • Two perpendicular number axes that have a common origin and the same unit segment.
Coordinates of a point
  • Definition - The location of an arbitrary point A (xA; yA) relative to the drawn coordinate system (Fig. 1) is determined by the numbers xA and yA. These numbers are called the coordinates of the point.
    • abscissa – xA is called the abscissa of point A.
    • the ordinate – yA is called the ordinate of point A.
